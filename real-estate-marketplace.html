Go From FOR SALE to SOLD

It is a marketplace that has been in the industry for many years. It focuses on making the process of buying, selling and renting homes easy, quick and cost-effectiveness. It offers a comprehensive selection of properties, facilitated by the integration of multiple MLS listings, to ensure a thorough and precise search experience.

Country

Target Audience

Homeowners, Buyers, Renters

Business Problem

Finding the right property can be a time-consuming and tedious process for buyers, sellers and renters, as they have to visit multiple Real Estate agents or websites to find the right match. This is where the client saw an opportunity to create a custom solution that addresses this problem.

The client with 20+ years of Real Estate expertise wanted to build a platform that integrated multiple MLS platforms to provide a comprehensive and convenient solution for customers that will allow them to search and find properties in one place, thus making the process of buying, selling or renting properties much easier and efficient.

Project Challenges

  • MLS Integrations

    Mapping & Integrating multiple MLS APIs available in different formats.

  • Database

    Migrating & Maintaining 400+ GB of unstructured data.

  • Performance

    Considering the huge data and loading of the database, some of the biggest challenges was to ensure a smooth user experience.

  • Privacy Concerns

    Real Estate professionals deal with confidential data. Therefore, companies must secure information while also preventing unauthorized access.

  • Data Security

    Managing critical information about properties and their owner details was crucial as keeping it secure at different security layers.

Facing Similar Challenge? Talk to Our Expert

Gozoom Technologies Solution

Gozoom Technologies developed a platform that integrated 37+ MLS APIs into the system and accomplished their Real Estate offline data into the online system by using a polygon-based map along with a Zipcode mechanism for avoiding duplication of the same properties

Application Features

  • MLS Integration: Created a dynamic engine that can integrate any MLS, currently having integration with over 37+ MLSs

  • Advance Search: Real Estate Marketplace is a comprehensive search engine that brings together virtually every "for sale by owner" listings with different search results based on Address, Sub-division, City and Zip-code

  • Listing Views: Offers two ways to view properties, either in Card-View or Map-View mode, utilizing a polygon-based map

  • Property Enquiry: One can request an enquiry or schedule an appointment for property visit

  • Agent/Broker Directory: Listings from agents and brokers, a directory of these professionals, with information such as their contact details, specialties, and listings

  • Security: Ensuring the security of user data which includes measures such as secure login, encrypted data transmission, and regular security updates

  • Commission Saving Calculator: Seller can simply calculate the saving of commission fees charged by agents

  • Subscription Plans: Designed to provide users with enhanced functionality and access to more resources that can help them successfully buy or sell properties
