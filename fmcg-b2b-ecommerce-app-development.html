An ideal e-channel to provide a better sales
and after sales services to customers.

Adiyat app is an online sales channel & distribution application specialized in FMCG field and the sole agent of Unilever in Sudan for Home Care, Personal Care, & Food Portfolio. Adiyat Marketing & Distribution is a high standard distribution industry of FMCG products in Sudan having 10 branches, 1000+ employees, 400 Sale Vehicles, 26000 Customers & 500+ SKU’s

Brief

Adiyat was the pioneer in the ordinary distribution model in Sudan, and they wanted to be the same when it comes to E-distribution

Solution

Gozoom Technologies developed a powerful app for quick and easy buying with a personalized customer experience for 26k+ potential customers

Outcome

200+ vendors onboarded so far, which speaks success of the application

Business Problem

Creating a robust and seamless B2B ecommerce solutions channel to provide better sales and after-sales services to customers.

High turnover consumer packaged goods characterize the Fast-moving consumer goods (FMCG) sector, i.e. goods produced, distributed, marketed and consumed within a short period. FMCG products that dominate today are detergents, toiletries, tooth cleaning products, cosmetics, etc. Unilever is one of the top companies in this market, and Adiyat is the only sole agent in Sudan to distribute its products.

Adiyat, through this Mobile App, wanted to open an e-channel to provide better sales and after-sales services to their customers. Hence, our client came up with a vision to develop a customized online sales channel for 26K+ potential customers/vendors to seamlessly provide bulk ordering of FMCG products. The application is personalized based on the vendor, industry, and portfolio shopping becomes a treat for the customer.

Project Challenges

Eliminating bottlenecks associated with supply chain,
customize order process and approval to build personalized B2B ecommerce solutions.

  • Personalized vendor based application
  • Offline payment mode for customers
  • Bulk order & management
  • Utilizing the consumer's purchasing preferences to make selling decisions
  • Personalizing the customer experience based on their Industry & portfolio
  • Managing the quarterly and annual targets for the customers
  • Mismanaged supply chain
  • 26k+ potential customers
  • Customized order process and approval
